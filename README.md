## ◈ About Me

I'm currently pursuing my **B.Tech in CSE (Data Science)** at **Malla Reddy Engineering College, Hyderabad**.

What began as a journey focused on learning and placements has evolved into a deeper purpose — **building meaningful, scalable, and high-impact solutions through technology.**

My foundation lies in **Data Structures & Algorithms (C++)**, **Full-Stack Development (MERN Stack)**, **SQL & NoSQL Database Design**, and performance-focused backend architecture.

I recently completed an internship with **Edunet Foundation** in collaboration with **EY GDS & AICTE**, gaining hands-on experience building scalable applications and understanding real-world engineering practices.

Hackathons and collaborative projects transformed my mindset — from just writing code to **thinking in systems, scalability, and innovation.**

> _Every conversation, every project, every failure is an opportunity. Learning never stops._
